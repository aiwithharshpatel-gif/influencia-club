## 🎯 Creator Feedback Questions

### Onboarding Experience (First 5 Minutes)

```
1. What was your FIRST impression when you landed on the site?

2. Was the registration process clear? 
   - What confused you?
   - What almost made you drop off?

3. How long did it take to complete your profile?
   - Was any step frustrating?
   - Did you skip any fields? Why?

4. What made you decide to sign up?
   - What was the "hook"?

5. On a scale of 1-5, how easy was onboarding?
   - If < 5, what would make it a 5?
```

### Platform Usage (Daily/Weekly Behavior)

```
6. How often do you check the Influenzia Club dashboard?
   - Daily / Weekly / Rarely / Never

7. What do you do when you log in?
   - Check points?
   - Browse brand deals?
   - Update profile?
   - Something else?

8. Which feature do you use MOST?
   - Why that one?

9. Which feature do you use LEAST?
   - Why don't you use it?

10. Have you shared your referral link yet?
    - If yes: How many people signed up?
    - If no: What's stopping you?
```

### Value Proposition (Core Benefits)

```
11. What's the #1 benefit you've gotten from Influenzia Club?

12. Have you earned any money through the platform yet?
    - If yes: How was the experience?
    - If no: What's blocking you?

13. How does Influenzia Club compare to other platforms you've used?
    - What do we do better?
    - What do competitors do better?

14. Would you recommend Influenzia Club to other creators?
    - Why or why not?

15. What would you tell a friend about Influenzia Club in ONE sentence?
```

### Pricing & Monetization

```
16. Would you pay ₹199/month for a Pro membership?

    - If no: Why not? What's a fair price?

17. How important are points and rewards to you?
    - Very / Somewhat / Not really / Not at all
```

## 💡 Pro Tips for Feedback Calls

### DO:
```
✅ Listen more than you talk
✅ Ask "why" at least 3 times per call
✅ Take verbatim notes (use their words)
✅ Record calls (with permission)
✅ Stay neutral (don't defend the product)
✅ Thank them sincerely
✅ Follow up within 24 hours
```

### DON'T:
```
❌ Pitch or sell during the call
❌ Explain why something is "actually good"
❌ Get defensive about criticism
❌ Lead the witness ("Don't you think X is great?")
❌ Interrupt
❌ Make promises you can't keep
❌ Skip the follow-up
```

## 📞 Call Structure (15 Minutes)

```
Minutes 0-2: Rapport Building
- Thanks for joining!
- How's your day going?
- Quick intro: Why we're doing this

Minutes 2-5: Background
- Tell me about yourself/your brand
- How did you find us?
- What were you hoping to achieve?

Minutes 5-12: Deep Dive
- Walk me through your experience
- What worked well?
- What was frustrating?
- Show me [specific feature]
- Why did you do that?

Minutes 12-14: Wrap-Up
- NPS question (0-10)
- One thing to improve?
- Can I follow up?

Minutes 14-15: Thank You
- Sincere thanks
- Next steps
- Send thank you email
```
